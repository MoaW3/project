Gameplay

Gameplay is without a doubt the biggest part of The Sims 4. The player has to take care of a household of 1-8 sims, make sure all their needs are met, that they have enough money as well as completing aspirations or challenges - depending on how you play the game, and the sims experience different emotions that affect their ability to do certain things.

CHEATS

"rosebud"
Gives the sim's household 1000 simoleons.
"motherlode"
Gives the sim's household 50 000 simoleons.
"freerealestate on"
Makes all lots free.
"careers.promote Careername"
Promotes the sim one step up in whatever career they have. Also works for school grades.
"sims.fill_all_commodities"
Fills needs for every sim in the household.
"Death.toggle"
Disables death in the game.
"modifyrelationship Firstname1 Lastname1 Firstname2 Lastname2 100 LTR_Friendship_Main"
Changes a relationship between two sims. In this example the sims' friendship went up to 100%.
"aspirations.complete_current_milestone"
Completes a sim's current aspiration milestone.

MODS

KawaiiStacie's Slice of Life Mod
Makes the sims more realistic and gives them more personality.
Weerbesu's UI Cheats Extension Mod
Makes cheats easier.
KawaiiStacie's Education System Bundle
Makes the education system in the Sims 4 better.
Deaderpool's MC Command Center
Gives the player a lot more control over how the game works and makes cheats easier
