Packs

There are 4 different types of content packs in the sims 4. Expansion packs, game packs, stuff packs and kits. Expansion packs contain the most content out of the four and focus on new game features as well as clothes, furniture, neighborhoods, worlds and life states. Game packs are similar to expansion packs, but are smaller and with less content. Stuff packs usually only include clothes and furniture - and only occasionally gameplay features. Kits are the smallest type of content packs and only come with a few objects and gameplay changes and were first released in 2021.

Expansion Packs

Get to Work (2015)

Added one skill, three playable careers, several new collectibles, aliens and a secret lot.

Get Together (2015)

Added clubs, cafés, two new traits, two new skills, a new aspiration, and a new world, Windenburg.

City Living (2016)

Added the city world San Myshuno, festivals, apartments, three new careers, one personality trait, several lot traits, one skill, a new aspiration, new collectibles, spices and rabbit-hole venues.

Cats and Dogs (2017)

Added pets (cats and dogs, specifically), vet clinics, a new world (Brindelton Bay), two new traits, multiple lot traits, two new skills, one new aspiration and several collectibles.

Seasons (2018)

Added weather, seasons, holidays, a calendar, several traits, two new skills, two new careers, multible new collectibles and a lot of new activities.

Get Famous (2018)

Added fame, reputation, one new trait, three lot traits, two skills, two aspirations and two careers.

Island Living (2019)

Added the new world Sulani, mermaids, two new traits, three lot traits, a new aspiration, a new career, three part-time jobs, several collectibles, oceans, functional beaches, suntanning/sunburning, volcanoes, odd jobs and new animals.

Discover University (2019)

Added two universities, as well as a system for degrees, a new world (Britechester), a new aspiration, two skills and 5 careers as well as a new life state and a roommate feature.

Eco Lifestyle (2020)

Added the new world Evergreen Harbor, an "eco footprint" feature, several new objects to make things like candles and juice, four new traits, two aspirations and a new career as well as two new skills and a new weather type - acid rain.

Snowy Escape (2020)

Added the world Mt. Komorebi, the new "lifestyle" system, two new traits, three skills, two aspirations, one career and is the first pack to include a world with both residential and vacation lots.

Cottage Living (2021)

Added several animals (cows, llamas, rabbits and foxes), a new world (Henford-on-Bagley), two traits, one aspiration, one skill, several collectibles (mainly plants), multible new activities and new recipes.

Game Packs

Outdoor Retreat (2015)

Added vacation lots, camping, one trait, aspiration and skill.

Spa Day (2015)

Added a new trait, three aspirations, a new venue and is mainly focused on wellness.

Dine Out (2016)

Added resturaunts to the game, as well as the ability for sims to own and manage them. They also introduced experimental food - recipes that can be learned after eating them.

Vampires (2017)

Added vampires, with the ability to turn other sims into vampires, become immortal as well as having supernatural powers. A new world, Forgotten Hollow, was also introduced.

Parenthood (2017)

Added the parenthoodhood skill for parents, and character values for sim toddlers, children and teenagers. The pack also includes private journals, school projects and a new aspiration.

Jungle Adventure (2018)

Added a new world, Selvadora, with ancient temples and artifacts.

StrangerVille (2019)

Added the new world StrangerVille - a desert town with a lot of secrets. The pack is mainly focused on a mystery that the sim has to solve. There is also a new career, aspiration and trait.

Realm of Magic (2019)

Added the magical world Glimmerbrook, s well as a separate, somewhat hidden, neighborhood - The Magic Realm. The pack also added spellcasters and familiars.

Journey to Batuu (2020)

This pack is a collaboration with the Star Wars franchise and added a new world: the planet Batuu. The player can create their own story by completing different missions. Four new aspirations and three traits were also added.

Dream Home Decorator (2021)

Added an active career and centers mainly around interior design and a new like/dislike category.

My Wedding Stories (2022)

Added the new world Tartosa, and centers around weddings and wedding preparations.
