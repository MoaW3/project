Build & Buy

Build & Buy is the part of the game where the player can build and decorate houses, parks or any type of area in the game. In basegame (without any extra content) there are three worlds: Willow Creek, Oasis Springs and Newcrest - the latter being a completely empty world.

CHEATS

"bb.moveobjects"
Allows objects to be place anywhere.
"bb.ignoregameplayunlocksentitlement"
Gives the player access to objects that otherwise has to be unlocked through gameplay.
"bb.showhiddenobjects"
Shows "hidden" objects, for example some plants, a lot of decoration and objects that are made in gameplay.
"bb.enablefreebuild"
Allows the player to build on any lot at any time.
